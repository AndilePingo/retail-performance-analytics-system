# Retail Performance Analytics System

## Client Problem

A multinational retail organization wants to better understand sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and regional performance.

Current challenges include:

* Difficulty identifying top-performing products.
* Limited visibility into regional profitability.
* Lack of centralized reporting.
* Inefficient decision-making due to fragmented data.

## Project Objectives

1. Create a reliable reporting system.
2. Identify revenue and profit drivers.
3. Analyze customer segments.
4. Evaluate regional performance.
5. Provide executive recommendations.

## Success Metrics

* Clear KPI reporting.
* Executive dashboard.
* Actionable business insights.
* Data quality validation.
* Reproducible analysis workflow.
