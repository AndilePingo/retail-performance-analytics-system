# Retail Performance Analytics System

## Project Overview

This project analyzes global retail sales data to identify revenue drivers, profitability trends, customer behavior patterns, and operational improvement opportunities.

The analysis was conducted using Python and business intelligence techniques to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights.

---

## Business Problem

A multinational retail company wants to better understand:

- Which markets generate the most revenue and profit
- Which product categories drive business performance
- Which customer segments are most valuable
- How discounts impact profitability
- Which products contribute most to gains and losses

The goal is to support data-driven decision-making through reporting, analytics, and dashboard development.

---

## Dataset

Dataset: Global Superstore Dataset

The dataset contains:

- 51,290 transactions
- 24 columns
- 25,035 unique orders
- 1,590 unique customers

Key fields include:

- Sales
- Profit
- Quantity
- Discount
- Market
- Region
- Category
- Customer Segment
- Shipping Information

## Data Quality Assessment

### Missing Values

Postal Code contains missing values for a large number of international records.

This was expected because not all countries use postal codes consistently.

### Duplicate Records

No duplicate records were identified.

### Data Types

Date fields were converted to datetime format for time-series analysis.

---

## Key Performance Indicators

| Metric | Value |
|----------|---------|
| Revenue | $12.64 Million |
| Profit | $1.47 Million |
| Orders | 25,035 |
| Customers | 1,590 |
| Profit Margin | 11.61% |

---

## Key Findings

### Market Performance

APAC generated the highest revenue and profit among all markets.

Top Markets:

1. APAC
2. European Union
3. United States

### Product Categories

Technology generated:

- Highest Revenue
- Highest Profit

Technology was identified as the strongest-performing category.

### Customer Segments

The Consumer segment generated:

- Highest Revenue
- Highest Profit

Consumer customers should remain a strategic focus area.

### Discount Analysis

A negative correlation (-0.316) was identified between discounts and profit.

This suggests excessive discounting may reduce profitability.

### Product Profitability

Several products generated significant losses, indicating opportunities for pricing, inventory, or promotional strategy improvements.

---

## Business Recommendations

### Recommendation 1

Increase investment in APAC markets through targeted marketing campaigns and inventory expansion.

### Recommendation 2

Prioritize Technology products due to their strong revenue and profitability performance.

### Recommendation 3

Review pricing and discount policies to reduce margin erosion.

### Recommendation 4

Investigate underperforming products and determine whether they should be repriced, repositioned, or discontinued.

### Recommendation 5

Focus customer acquisition efforts on Consumer segment customers.

---

The dashboard provides insights into:
